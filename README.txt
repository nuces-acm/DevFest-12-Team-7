
Abstract:
	Our domain of development is “Voting & Election”. We are implementing an idea to automate the voting system of Pakistan.
	For this purpose, at this point we are making a Web Based and Android application, but planned to extend it to all platforms.
	Our Automated Voting System (AVS) will automate the current voting system of Pakistan and provide different functionalities and 
	opportunities to its user to provide more ease and tension free + fair voting environment.
